One Africa Alliance Elder Care & Support Program

Caring for Our Elders with Respect, Health, and Dignity

Older adults are the pillars of our communities, carrying wisdom, culture, and legacy. Yet many elders face challenges such as social isolation, health issues, and financial insecurity. The One Africa Alliance Elder Care & Support Program is dedicated to ensuring that Africaâ€™s elders live with the dignity, care, and respect they deserve.

We provide holistic support that addresses health, social inclusion, economic empowerment, and safety to improve the quality of life for elders and their families.

Why Elder Care Matters

Our elders have nurtured generations and built communities. Supporting them enriches society as a whole, preserves cultural heritage, and promotes intergenerational solidarity.

Our Pillars of Support

1. Health & Wellness

  • Facilitating access to affordable and quality healthcare services.
  • Promoting nutrition and wellness programs tailored for elders.
  • Supporting mental health and cognitive care initiatives.

2. Social Inclusion & Community Engagement

  • Creating safe spaces and community centers for elders.
  • Encouraging participation in cultural, educational, and recreational activities.
  • Reducing social isolation through outreach and peer support.

3. Economic Empowerment

  • Providing opportunities for elders to engage in income-generating activities.
  • Offering financial literacy and resource management training.
  • Assisting with pension access and social safety nets.

4. Safety and Protection

  • Advocating against elder abuse and neglect.
  • Providing legal aid and awareness about elder rights.
  • Promoting safe living environments and emergency response support.

5. Family Support & Caregiver Assistance

  • Supporting families and caregivers with training and resources.
  • Facilitating respite care services.
  • Promoting awareness of elder care needs within families and communities.

Our Key Programs

Community Elder Centers

Safe, welcoming spaces offering social activities, health screenings, and counseling.

Health Access Outreach

Mobile clinics and health education for elders in remote areas.

Elder Economic Empowerment

Skills training and micro-grants for elder-led businesses and cooperatives.

Legal & Protection Services

Support for elder rights, abuse prevention, and legal counseling.

Caregiver Support Network

Training, respite, and resources for those caring for elders.

How You Can Help

  • Donors and Partners: Fund programs that uplift and protect elders.
